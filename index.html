Career



2016 > 2017 - BLACK CAT STUDIO as Freelance Game Designer


During my time within the fresh new studio, I worked on two unnanounced mobile games developed with Unity.

I arrived on the first project, based on an existing dice game, to help the team finish it. I mainly worked on:

  • The design and implemantation of the tutorial
  • The design and balance of the Achievement and rewards system
  • The design and documentation of new features
  • Giving feedbacks and suggestions to the team on the UIX
 On the second one, I was the game designer from the start to the end of the project. Here are the main tasks i did:

  • Working on the concept of game and producing the necessary documentation for the team
  • Designing all the systems and features
  • Creating and implementing numerous level
  • Assisting the producer as team co-manager
  • Working closely with the artist to create the UIX and feedbacks
  • Balancing the whole experience


2014 > 2016 - FUNKADELICHIK as Game Designer


My second and longest professional experience as an employee. The company's aim was to develop games that, within the player experience, work on re-establish the bond between citizens and their representative. We mainly targeted the web and mobile plateforms.

As the company's only game designer, I did a lot of different tasks:

  • Conceive the design behind any systems and game mechanics for all the studio's projects
  • Managing and leading the production team for several projects
  • Introducing agile methods and good pratices for the pipeline within the studio
  • Programming in javascript - using Phaser - whole projects
  • Presenting documentation and progress to the different clients


2013 - FUNKADELICHIK as Freelance Game Designer


Before becoming their first employee, I worked with the founders of Funkadelichik as a Freelancer. I did various missions:

  • Brainstorming, presenting and conceiving several concepts to respond the company's intentions
  • Working remotly in coop with a programmer to produce two games
  • Participate in several brainstorming session about the company strategy and futur


2011 - ALTERNATIVE SHIFT as Game Designer trainee


I joined the altshift Team as an junior game designer. It was my final internship before graduating. At that time, they were working on Besyde, a MMO based on geolocalisation and reality augmented.

There was only one other game designer on the project. Consequently, I quickly was considered as a full game designer, resulting in one of my favorite professional experience.

In cooperation with the other game designer first, I worked on:

  • Brainstorming, presenting and conceiving several concepts to respond the company's intentions
  • Working remotly in coop with a programmer to produce two games
  • Participate in several brainstorming session about the company strategy and futur


2011 - Graduate from Supinfogame in Game Design and Management

During 4 years at Supinfogame, we acquire knowledge about game design theory, exchange with active professionnal and made a lots of games: from levels to full-pledge games.

  • Learn Game engines like UDK and Unity
