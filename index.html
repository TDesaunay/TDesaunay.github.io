IPSEGO

  • Company: Funkadelichik
  • Date: 2015-2016
  • Job: Game Design

The idea & ambition behind that 6 months project was quite the challenge : design a game that reach the player about her-his principles/values in order to let her/him discover or confirm them.

After a few meeting, we decided to make a point'n'click adventure. Narrative Design was, at that moment, the quickest and more efficient way to play & experience those values.

Previous Next

We quickly decided on the core concept: the player is send to explore an extra dimensional world where the inhabitant are living representations of groups of principles based on the work of Shalom H. Swchartz. By interacting with them, making choice, s-he will discover her/his own values.

Orleanoide

  • Company: Funkadelichik
  • Date: 2014
  • Job: Game Design & Programmer

One month length project for the science festival of Orleans. The constraint were to realize a game that use the mascot of the festival (the orleanoid), a popular core gameplay and introduce a serious aspect in it.

Previous Next

The first two constraint were easily responded. We proposed an dual stick shooter with a little narrative twist. You shoot some unidentified enemies in order to save little friendly orleanoids. If you messed up or saved enough of them, the great One asks you a question about the ongoing festival.


Tower Defense

  • Company: Funkadelichik
  • Date: 2014
  • Job: Game Design & Programmer

This game was born from an intern challenge. 3 weeks to design 3 projects. That was one of them. It was designed & built around one of our client important theme: Pollution. More precisely: Waste Sorting.

Waste sorting is all about different way to collect & treat trash. I had to imagine which existing core gameplay will be capable to represent the abstract system of waste sorting.

Previous Next

Tower Defense was the answer. That's how you obtain a game where you have to install lovely little waste treatment plants that try to stop waves of different types of trashes to end in the open dump. Save your town !


Funky Crush

  • Client: Funkadelichik
  • Date: 2013
  • Job: Game Design

One of the very first project I did when I joined Funkadelichik. The company wanted to have a game from a very popular existing genre. They decided to go for a candid crush like game.

So I did some research around tile-matching games. Internet has a lot of interesting stuff about it. Like this incredible paper from Jesper Juul.

Previous Next

As a result, we did our version in a little less than 3 months. We stuck to the arcade style and included time based events like weekly/monthly scoring systems.


Besyde: Mask of Harmony

  • Company: Alt Shift
  • Date: 2011
  • Job: Game Designer

Developed by Alt Shift, Besyde is a MMO using geo-localization and augmented reality to brought to life an alternative dimension. A world where creatures live in gigantic trees, hiding from monster. As a player, you can explore that world walking around your city (geo-localization) or from your home using a special portal (facebook).

Previous Next

The project was ongoing for several months when I arrived as a trainee. I mainly worked on the new UI, the monetization system and some new explorations features. It was one of the most enjoyable professional experience I had.
